# OJT Portfolio Guidelines

## General Reminders
All students enrolled in the On-the-job Training (OJT) program are required to submit both hard and soft copies of their OJT Portfolio as part of their course completion.

Ensure that all required documents are complete, accurate, and well-organized.

Observe professionalism in formatting and content.

## Soft Copy Requirements
The soft copy must be submitted in PDF format.

The filename format must be: LastName_FirstName_OJT Portfolio.
Example: Santos_Ana_OJT Portfolio

The soft copy must include:
- Cover Page
- Table of Contents
- Checklist of Requirements
- Notarized MOA
- Endorsement Letter
- Internship Agreement
- Consent Form
- Comprehensive Resume/Profile
- Daily Attendance Report (DTR) duly signed by the Training Supervisor
- Weekly Accomplishment or Progress Report or Learning Diary
- Professional Readings (Optional)
- Certificates from networking and linkages participation such as local, national, or international webinars, trainings, and conferences (Optional)
- Evaluation Forms accomplished by the Employer or Training Supervisor
- Evaluation Forms accomplished by the Student-Intern for the HTE and Training Supervisor
- Internship Experience Summary Report
- Photo Documentation of the Actual Training or Output
- Certificate of Completion issued by HTE

## Hard Copy Requirements
All OJT requirements should be printed on legal bond paper.

Use a long folder with a transparent cover.

All pages should be clean, readable, and properly labeled.

The folder color depends on the department:
- OM and IT - Purple
- Computer and Electronics - Blue
- Electrical and Mechanical - Yellow
- Civil and Railway - Black
