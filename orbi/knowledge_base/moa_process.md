# MOA Process for HTE Without an Existing MOA

## MOA Steps
If the Host Training Establishment (HTE) does not yet have an existing MOA, the process usually follows these steps:

1. New Potential HTE
2. Submit the MOA to HTE
3. MOA to OJT Coordinator
4. MOA to Legal Approval
5. Approved MOA
6. Printing of MOA
7. MOA for Signature
8. Informing of Retrieval
9. Retrieval of MOA
10. Signature of HTE in MOA and Notarization
