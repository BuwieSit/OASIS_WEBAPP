# OJT Requirements

## Required Portfolio Contents
The OJT Portfolio must contain the following required documents:

- Cover Page
- Table of Contents
- Checklist of Requirements
- Notarized MOA
- Endorsement Letter
- Internship Agreement
- Consent Form
- Comprehensive Resume or Profile
- Daily Attendance Report (DTR) duly signed by the Training Supervisor
- Weekly Accomplishment Report, Progress Report, or Learning Diary
- Evaluation Forms accomplished by the Employer or Training Supervisor
- Evaluation Forms accomplished by the Student-Intern for the HTE and Training Supervisor
- Internship Experience Summary Report
- Photo Documentation of the Actual Training or Output
- Certificate of Completion issued by the HTE

## Optional Documents
The following may also be included if available:

- Professional Readings
- Certificates from networking and linkages participation such as local, national, or international webinars, trainings, or conferences

## Submission Format
Students are required to submit:
- One soft copy in PDF format
- One hard copy printed on legal bond paper

## Soft Copy Filename Format
Use this format when naming the PDF file:

LastName_FirstName_OJT Portfolio

Example:
Santos_Ana_OJT Portfolio
