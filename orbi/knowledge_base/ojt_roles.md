# OJT Roles and Responsibilities

## Student-Intern
The student-intern is responsible for:
- Attending the Pre-internship Orientation
- Submitting the Intent Letter
- Enrolling in the internship course
- Attending consultations and monitoring
- Attending HTE Orientation
- Submitting required reports
- Beginning the internship on time
- Reporting issues promptly
- Preparing and submitting the complete OJT Portfolio
- Answering the post-OJT student feedback form

## Training Supervisor or Employer
The Training Supervisor or Employer is responsible for:
- Signing the Daily Attendance Report (DTR)
- Accomplishing evaluation forms for the student-intern
- Overseeing the student during the internship period as required by the training setup

## Internship Adviser
The internship adviser is responsible for:
- Receiving the student's portfolio after internship
- Reviewing submitted requirements
- Supporting final evaluation and grade computation

## OJT Coordinator and Related Offices
The OJT Coordinator and related offices are involved in:
- Processing MOA documents
- Coordinating approvals
- Facilitating legal review and documentation steps when needed
